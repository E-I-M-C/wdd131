Sights

The state of Baja California Norte is in the northern part of the peninsula that has the same name, between two bodies of crystalline water: the Pacific to the west and the Gulf of California to the east. Discover beaches, caves and breakwaters, explore small towns and huge canyons, and enjoy the high energy and excitement of cities.

To get to Baja California Norte, you can take a flight to the international airports of Mexicali and Tijuana. There are six border crossings from Baja California into the United States, and trucks pass back and forth several times a day. Visit Tijuana in October to sample the delights of the Baja California Culinary Festival, held every year.
